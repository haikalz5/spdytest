Spdy Parcel - Service Details
  • Collection and delivery services are available every day of the week.
  • Book before 2:45 PM for same-day pickup.
  • Pickup times are between 1 PM and 6 PM.
  • Delivery will be completed within 3 business days.
  • Delivery is unavailable to P.O. boxes and certain remote areas.
  • Maximum weight: 50 kg.
  • Maximum dimensions: No single side can exceed 150 cm, and the total (L + W + H) must be less than 280 cm.
  • Parcel weight will be determined by actual weight or volumetric weight, whichever is higher.
  • Volumetric weight formula: (Height × Width × Length) cm / 6000.
  • All prices are inclusive of shipping fees but exclude additional courier surcharges.
  • RM200 insurance coverage is included.
  • Additional RM50 insurance coverage is available for lost parcels.

By clicking "Confirm & Book", you agree to Spdy Parcel's terms, including the restricted items list and non-compensation policies.
